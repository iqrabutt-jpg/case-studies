📈 August In Numbers 📈

Headline Commercials 🤑

OV: 167.9k (-9.4% MoM)

GMV: £4.9m (-9.1% MoM)

AOV: £29.24 (+0.14p MoM)

TM KPIs 📊

Inactive Churn: 4.5% (+0.1pp MoM)

Menu Completeness: 77.5% (+1% MoM)

Ads Revenue: £103k (9.5% of CL Ads)

Signage: 168 logged QTD

August proved to be a challenging month for the portfolio as OV and GMV tracked below Core London’s overall August numbers (-7.1% OV & -7.2%). The portfolio did see a larger amount of restaurants closed over the holiday period with restaurant count down -2.6% vs CL -1.2%. In addition, we lost one of our Top 20 brands, Macro Made, to a legal and compliance issue.

 

August Wins🏆

  • AEGIS wins: Clapton Table (Gold) & KOYN Thai (Gold) 💛

  • Fait Maison's Churn Prevention: following issues relating to their M+ switch we were able to prevent the Partner churning to UE 🚲

  • We put up 94 pieces of signage in SMB accounts 🏪


August Misses🤔

  • We abandoned Five Fingers, Hop Vietnamese and OTE negotiations but all remain on the platform ❌

  • The team did not hit the 100% RCI target set, currently performing at 90.5% 🏍️

  • Menu Completeness lagged behind the target and we are now focusing on supporting Partners via photoshoots and best practice 📸

 

Insight of the Month 🔎

Whilst out and about in zone, Brian has noticed a big change happening in Peckham. From new developments to being named in London’s top 10  poshest neighbourhoods to live in by the Telegraph, Brian is seeing loads of new restaurants and tonnes of potential.


Brian will be visiting the area with Will Newton, a Commercial Manager in the Incubator Pod, to build out a plan for capturing as much orders and market share as possible.
